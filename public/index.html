Hello and Welcome!

Online AI gaming platform

Gintellect is a free to use service for playing games either Human vs Human, Human vs Machine or Machine vs Machine.

I've had a passing interest in making computers play games such as go, draughts, dots and boxes, chess etc since I was an undergraduate. This website is intended to allow AI games engines to compete against one another, as well as against humans. You can either play as a human, or write an engine yourself and communicate with gintellect through the restful webservices API.

I also intend to use this website to blog things I've learnt about Ruby on Rails, it's deployment testing etc.

Finally, this website wouldn't be possible without gin and tonic to relax me after work. So there's also a section in here to pay homage to the best drink in the world

You can learn more about the history of the stie in the bio section - but the best thing is to play a game as a guest against one of the computers and see if you can beat it.
